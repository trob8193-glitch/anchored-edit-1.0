# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-06

Anchored collects and processes limited account, location, and gameplay data to provide app functionality.

## Data We Collect

- Account data: user ID, display name, email (if provided via sign-in).
- App data: profile preferences, in-app activity, and feature usage.
- Location data: precise and coarse location to power territory and map gameplay.
- Diagnostics: crash and performance telemetry if enabled in production builds.

## How We Use Data

- Provide core gameplay and map-based interactions.
- Enable account authentication and session continuity.
- Improve reliability, performance, and product quality.
- Prevent abuse and protect service integrity.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal data.
- Data may be processed by infrastructure providers required to operate the app.

## Data Retention

- Data is retained only as needed for app operations, legal obligations, and abuse prevention.

## Your Choices

- You can disable location permissions from device settings.
- You can request account/data deletion through support channels.

## Security

- We apply reasonable technical and organizational controls to protect data.

## Children

- The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the age required by local law).

## Contact

- For privacy requests, use your support contact channel for the app deployment.
